We Are Linac

We are company builders.
We pursue valuable patents and IP, find domain-specific entrepreneurs, and invest seed funds.

How we do it

We work in 3 simple steps

Identify

Identify top-notch academic research in the form of patents or proprietary technology* from leading academic institutions. We help to find and validate a strong business use-case.


Build Team

We build a team of experienced entrepreneurs in the relevant industry, coming from both business and technology backgrounds. All with full involvement of the academic researcher and the academic institution.


Invest

We provide up to $250k to jumpstart the company and allow the team to build a POC (Proof-of-Concept) that can demonstrate the value of the technology and the business model.

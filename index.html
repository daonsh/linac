Hello There

We Are Linac3

Imagine you could get superb 3D printed parts at the first try. Wouldn't it be a bliss? We enable just that, a true revolution in the way 3D printers operate, saving material, time to market and manufacturing better products.

How we do it

We work in 3 simple steps

Identify

Our machine vision engine identifies mistakes as they happen


Change Printing Plan

Our engine analyzes the error and creates modified printing commands


Fix

The new commands are executed by the printer on the fly, correcting the errors and preemptively preventing them from accumulating
